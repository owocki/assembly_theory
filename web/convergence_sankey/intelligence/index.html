About Intelligence Evolution

Intelligence—the ability to process information, learn, and adapt—has evolved multiple times through different mechanisms. This visualization shows how various lineages developed sophisticated information processing capabilities.

  • Vertebrate brains: Centralized neural processing
  • Cephalopod intelligence: Distributed neural networks
  • Arthropod cognition: Miniaturized efficiency
  • Collective intelligence: Emergent from simple agents
  • Artificial intelligence: Silicon-based computation
