The Assembly Theory Lens

Assembly theory, when applied to Ethereum and smart contract composability, reveals profound insights about how complexity emerges in decentralized systems. Just as biological organisms evolve through iterative assembly of molecular components, smart contracts combine and compose to create increasingly sophisticated financial and computational structures.

This framework allows us to quantify complexity, predict evolutionary paths, assess systemic risks, and design more efficient protocols. By understanding the assembly indices of different components, we can navigate the vast design space of possible blockchain applications more effectively.
