ASSEMBLY COMPLEXITY PROGRESSION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Assembly Index Scale:
────────────────────
    10¹  │ Simple molecules (H₂O, CO₂)
    10²  │ Amino acids, nucleotides
    10³  │ Proteins, RNA/DNA strands
    10⁴  │ Basic cellular machinery
    10⁵  │ Complete prokaryotic cells
    10⁶  │ Eukaryotic cells with organelles
    10⁷  │ Simple multicellular organisms
    10⁸  │ Complex body plans, organs
    10⁹  │ Advanced nervous systems
    10¹⁰ │ Social organisms, basic cognition
    10¹¹ │ Advanced cognition, tool use
    10¹² │ Language, culture, technology (Humans)

Key Transitions in Assembly Complexity:
─────────────────────────────────────
1. Abiotic → Biotic (RNA World)
2. Prokaryotic → Eukaryotic (Endosymbiosis)
3. Unicellular → Multicellular (Cell specialization)
4. Simple → Complex multicellular (Cambrian explosion)
5. Instinctual → Cognitive (Brain evolution)
6. Biological → Cultural evolution (Human emergence)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
Each level represents exponential increases in assembly complexity, with reuse and hierarchical
combination of previous assemblies enabling the emergence of ever more complex forms of life.
